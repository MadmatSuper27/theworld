Guest Information

Everything you need to know about your stay at The World Apartment.

How do I check in?

We use an Easy Online Check-In system. You'll receive a secure link 24 hours before arrival to provide your info and get your digital access code.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM onwards. Check-out is by 10:00 AM.

Is parking available?

Yes, there are several parking options nearby, including on-street parking and secure garages. We can provide details upon booking.
